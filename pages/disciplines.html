How We Work

THE PROCESS

01

Discovery

We actively seek out artists working in unexpected ways — through open calls, referrals, and our own research into emerging voices globally. We look for work that has a distinct visual language and a genuine point of view.

02

Selection

Each application is reviewed by the full curatorial team. We assess not just the quality of the work, but its originality, consistency, and potential — asking whether this is a practice that will grow and challenge convention.

03

Collaboration

Once in the collective, artists work with our editorial team to develop their presence on the platform — curating portfolio selections, writing statements, and identifying opportunities for collaboration across disciplines.

04

Amplification

We connect artists with the right audiences — commissioning editors, galleries, brands, and institutions who are looking for exactly the kind of bold work our collective produces. This is where career-defining opportunities are made.
