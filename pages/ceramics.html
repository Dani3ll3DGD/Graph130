The Journey

FROM EARTH
TO OBJECT

01

Wedging

Clay is kneaded to remove air pockets and align the mineral particles — the meditative foundation of every piece.

02

Forming

Thrown on the wheel or built by hand — the clay takes shape through pressure, patience, and intuition.

03

Bisque Fire

The first kiln firing hardens the clay into a porous state, ready to accept glaze and survive the final heat.

04

Glaze & Fire

Glaze transforms in flame — what goes in as powder comes out as glass. The kiln has the final word.
